The Place for Fully Encrypted Team Communications.

For businesses of all sizes who realize the importance of keeping their work processes, client project information, and conversations about intellectual property safe, at all times.
Free for as long as you want.



Email is endangering your security

Email is notorious for being one of the least secure methods of communication across the internet. Almost all email providers such as Gmail and Outlook are scanning and accessing content held within emails. Zebu Connect uses military grade encryption across the entire platform to protect your teams work. End-to-end, no knowledge encryption solution ensures that no one (not even us), nor the government or hackers can get your data.

Secure Client Conversations

Send any file safely and securely internally between teams, or even with clients. Your files are encrypted even before they are sent. Knowing your communications are fully encrypted means the ability to tell your clients that their projects are safe with you.

Even Files Are Encrypted

Not only do we encrypt your message, we also encrypt the media you send within your conversations. We’ll only decrypt them for you upon delivery, and when you give us the okay.

Can’t be Opened by Us

Unfortunately, while big tech giants might encrypt messages between you and someone else, they’re usually still able to read the message themselves. Meaning your chat provider has access to your communications data for learning, analysis, and more. We ensure its encrypted even against us, so you know your data will never be sold off.
